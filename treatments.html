Body Treatments FAQ

What types of facials do you offer?

We offer a variety of facials tailored to different skin types and concerns, including hydrating, anti-aging, and deep-cleansing facials.

How often should I get a massage?

The frequency of massages depends on your individual needs and lifestyle. Generally, a monthly massage is recommended for maintenance and relaxation.

What should I do to prepare for a waxing appointment?

Ensure your hair is at least a quarter-inch long for effective waxing. Avoid exfoliating or using lotions on the day of your appointment.

Are your body treatments suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes, we offer treatments specifically designed for sensitive skin. Please inform us of any skin concerns or allergies when booking your appointment.
